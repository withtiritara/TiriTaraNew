Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Welcome to Tiri Tara ("we," "our," "us").

We're committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

By visiting our website, filling our forms, or participating in our trips, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that helps us understand you better and curate meaningful experiences.

This includes:

  • Personal details: name, email, phone number, and location.
  • Travel preferences and responses: details shared through our vibe form or trip sign-ups.
  • Payment details: when booking a trip (processed securely via third-party payment partners; we do not store card data).
  • Communications: messages or feedback you send us by email, WhatsApp, or social media.
  • Website data: analytics, cookies, and browsing activity to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Curate and personalise trips that match your vibe and preferences.
  • Contact you regarding your application, invites, and bookings.
  • Share updates, newsletters, and community information (only with your consent).
  • Improve our services, website experience, and communications.
  • Process payments securely and manage your reservations.

We only use your information for the purpose you've shared it with us — no spam, no misuse.

3. Communication & Marketing

When you fill the Vibe Form or join the Tiri Tribe, you agree that we may contact you via:

  • Email for trip invites, updates, or stories.
  • WhatsApp for community announcements or personal invites.

You can opt out anytime by replying "STOP" or clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email.

4. Data Protection & Security

  • Your information is stored securely and accessed only by authorised Tiri Tara team members.
  • We use trusted third-party services (e.g., Zoho, Google Workspace, Razorpay) with industry-standard encryption.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with unrelated third parties.

5. Cookies & Website Analytics

  • Our website may use cookies and analytics tools (like Google Analytics or Meta Pixel) to understand visitor behaviour and improve user experience.
  • These tools collect general information — not personal details.
  • You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal information.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for communications.

For any privacy-related requests, please contact us at withtiritara@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Our site may contain links to other websites or services (like Instagram, payment portals, or booking pages).
  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites — please review their policies before sharing any personal information.

8. Updates to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements.
  • Any updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
