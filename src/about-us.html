The Story of Tiri Tara

Our name comes from Sanskrit: "Tiri" meaning journey, and "Tara" meaning a star. Together, they stand for what we aspire to be - a guiding star in your journey.

It all started with the passion to travel, and our kind of travel is really different, we want to travel like a traveller, not a tourist. All you should need to know is that we will take care of your travel, accommodation (we would want you to stay at home stays at the places we go to, but trust us, we don't wanna compromise on giving you the best experience), and food (our idea is for you to get the local food). Hence, a curation with us would include the travel+stay+food plus the very best thing - it will include you to have the best time.

It's not luxury travel or checklist tourism; it's about feeling alive in the unknown. We want to curate trips that will help you experience the places we travel to as it is.

We want to bring together curious souls, people who seek connection, not just destinations. Those who find home in conversations around a bonfire, laughter under starlit skies, and stories that stay long after the trip ends.
