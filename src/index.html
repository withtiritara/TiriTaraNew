What is Tiri Tara

We're Tiri Tara and we're Travel Curators.


We're an exclusive travel community where you're invited on soulful experiences filled with wonder, you'll know just 40% of the itinerary, and the rest unfolds as a surprise along the journey. We take you to places. We help you rediscover wonder, connect deeply, explore the cultures of the place, and meet like-minded strangers who just might become your future best friends.


We aspire to be a Tara in the Tiri of your life.

How to join

We plan just enough to keep you safe, and leave the rest open to discovery, thats why we only reveal 40% of the journey with you because the best stories are the ones that you dont know yet (dont worry, you'll know just enough)
